Built for the people in the field.

YourCityRoute is a free mobile route planner for anyone who works a stop list — city inspectors, code enforcement officers, utility techs, pest control professionals, property managers, and field workers of all kinds.

Each city version is pre-loaded with local agency contacts, ordinance references, court links, citation-issuing departments, and a curated resources directory built specifically for workers operating in that city.

No login. No account. No cloud. Every stop you add, every note you write, and every metric your shift generates stays on your device. Export it how you want, when you want.
❤ This app is funded by local government sponsorships and listings on the in-app resources page — no pop-up ads or banners. Just the information you need, where you know to look.
